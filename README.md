# SKR & SKR GCW(A) Kadapa – AAA Dashboard 2025-26

An interactive **Academic and Administrative Audit (AAA) Dashboard** developed for **SKR & SKR Government College for Women (Autonomous), Kadapa**.
The dashboard visually presents institutional data such as academics, faculty, students, infrastructure, research, placements, governance, and accreditation metrics in a modern and responsive web interface.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The **AAA Dashboard** is a web-based visualization tool designed to present the key institutional indicators required for:

* Academic and Administrative Audit (AAA)
* NAAC documentation
* Institutional data presentation
* Administrative decision support
* Public information display

The dashboard integrates **statistics, charts, tables, and progress indicators** to provide a clear overview of the college’s performance and achievements.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Present **institutional performance data visually**
* Support **AAA and NAAC documentation**
* Provide a **single interface for academic statistics**
* Enable quick access to **key performance indicators**
* Improve **data transparency and reporting**

## 📊 Dashboard Modules

The dashboard includes the following sections:

### 1️⃣ Overview

Displays the institutional profile including:

* Establishment year
* Total programmes
* Student enrollment
* Teaching staff
* MoUs
* Pass percentage
* Accreditation status

Includes charts for:

* Student enrollment trends
* Programme distribution
* Pass rate analysis
* Faculty composition
* Placement overview

---

### 2️⃣ Academics

Provides details on:

* Undergraduate and postgraduate programmes
* Value-added courses
* Programme distribution (BA, BSc, BCom)
* Student examination performance
* Pass percentage

---

### 3️⃣ Faculty

Displays faculty statistics such as:

* Total teaching staff
* PhD qualifications
* Faculty categories
* Academic contributions

---

### 4️⃣ Students

Shows student-related information including:

* Total enrollment
* Scholarships
* Internships
* Academic achievements

---

### 5️⃣ Infrastructure

Includes details about:

* Library resources
* Laboratories
* ICT-enabled classrooms
* Campus facilities
* Digital learning infrastructure

---

### 6️⃣ Research & MoUs

Displays:

* Research activities
* MoUs with institutions and industries
* Collaborative programmes
* Research output

---

### 7️⃣ Placements

Provides information on:

* Companies visited
* Students placed
* Internship participation
* Career guidance activities

---

### 8️⃣ Awards & Achievements

Highlights:

* Institutional awards
* Faculty achievements
* Student accomplishments
* National rankings

---

### 9️⃣ Governance

Contains information related to:

* Institutional administration
* Committees and governance structure
* Policy implementation

---

### 🔟 NAAC & AAA

Includes:

* Accreditation milestones
* AAA indicators
* Quality benchmarks
* Institutional rankings
